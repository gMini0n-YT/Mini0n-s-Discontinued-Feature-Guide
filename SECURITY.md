
# Reporting Security Issues

The Mini0n's Discontinued Feature Guide and it's community take security bugs in the guide seriously. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security issue, please use msg gMini0n's email: gmini0ngames@gmail.com

The Discontinued Features team will send a response indicating the next steps in handling your report. After the initial reply to your report, the security team will keep you informed of the progress towards a fix and full announcement, and may ask for additional information or guidance.
